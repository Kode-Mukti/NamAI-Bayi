# 30 — Development Roadmap

## Summary

Complete development roadmap from idea to production and beyond. Four phases covering MVP, Growth, Ecosystem, and Expansion over 36 months.

## Reasoning

A clear roadmap aligns the team, investors, and stakeholders on what to build and when. It prevents scope creep, ensures strategic focus, and communicates progress. Every phase has measurable outcomes.

## Recommendation

### Phase 1: MVP (Month 1-4)

**Theme**: Validate the core idea
**Budget**: Minimal ($500 total)
**Team**: 1 developer (full-stack)

| Milestone | Date | Deliverable | Success Criteria |
|-----------|------|-------------|-----------------|
| Project Kickoff | Week 1 | Architecture docs, design system | 30 documents complete |
| Sprint 1-2 | Week 1-4 | Home + Input screens | Navigation flow complete |
| Sprint 3-4 | Week 5-8 | AI engine + Result screen | AI generates quality names |
| Sprint 5-6 | Week 9-12 | Share + Wishlist + Web | Viral features working |
| Beta Launch | Week 13-14 | Private beta (20 users) | Bug-free core experience |
| **Public Launch** | **Week 15-16** | **Play Store + Web** | **10,000 MAU target** |

**Key Decisions**:
- Gemini 1.5 Flash as AI provider
- No user registration required
- Client-side storage only
- Freemium model (free only at launch)

**KPIs**:
- MAU: 10,000 by month 3
- Session duration: 3+ min
- Share rate: 30%
- App rating: 4.5+

### Phase 2: Growth (Month 5-8)

**KPIs**:

- Premium conversion: 3-5%
- Monthly revenue: Rp 50M+
- Organic traffic: 50% of total

### Contingency Plan

| Scenario | Probability | Response |
|----------|------------|----------|
| AI cost too high | Medium | Switch to Gemini Flash, optimize prompts, cache |
| User growth slower than expected | Medium | Double down on SEO, content marketing, partnerships |
| Competitor copies concept | High | Differentiate on cultural depth, brand trust, quality |
| Developer burnout | Medium | Reduce scope, extend timeline, hire help |
| Funding needed | Low | Bootstrap, apply for startup grants, angel investment |

## Implementation

- Execute Phase 1 (MVP) with full focus
- Review roadmap quarterly
- Adjust based on data, not intuition
- Celebrate milestones publicly
- Keep technical debt low for speed

## Future Improvement

- Formal advisory board for roadmap direction
- Community-voted features
- Open-source non-core components
- Annual offsite for strategy planning
- External audit of roadmap vs. execution
