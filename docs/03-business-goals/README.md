# 03 — Business Goals

## Summary

Six primary business goals drive NamAI Bayi: establish Kode Mukti branding, create an entry point for the AI ecosystem, grow organic traffic, encourage sharing, maximize engagement, and build a foundation for premium features.

## Reasoning

NamAI Bayi is not a standalone product. It is the first touchpoint in Kode Mukti's AI product family. Every goal is designed to build brand equity, user trust, and organic growth before monetization. The secondary goals ensure long-term ecosystem expansion.

## Recommendation

### Primary Goals (MVP - Year 1)

| # | Goal | KPI | Target |
|---|------|-----|--------|
| 1 | Establish Kode Mukti branding | Brand recall in target demographic | 70% of users associate NamAI Bayi with Kode Mukti |
| 2 | Entry point for AI ecosystem | Cross-app conversion rate | 15% of users try next Kode Mukti product |
| 3 | Grow organic traffic | Monthly active users (MAU) | 50,000 MAU by month 12 |
| 4 | Encourage sharing | Shares per session | 0.5 shares per session average |
| 5 | Maximize engagement | Session duration | 3+ minutes average session |
| 6 | Support Premium features | Premium conversion rate | 5% of active users |

### Secondary Goals (Year 2+)

| # | Goal | Description |
|---|------|-------------|
| 1 | Build AI ecosystem | Expand from naming to parenting AI tools |
| 2 | Cross-promote future apps | Use NamAI Bayi as distribution channel |
| 3 | Global expansion | Adapt for other cultural naming contexts |
| 4 | Data insights | Anonymous naming trend analysis |
| 5 | Strategic partnerships | Baby product brands, hospitals, parenting communities |

## Implementation

- Track all goals through Amplitude or PostHog analytics
- A/B test all viral features to optimize sharing rates
- Monthly business reviews against KPI targets
- User surveys at 3, 6, 12 months post-launch

## Future Improvement

- Add B2B API for hospitals and maternity clinics
- White-label solution for baby product brands
- Naming trend reports as content marketing
- Premium enterprise features for baby product companies
