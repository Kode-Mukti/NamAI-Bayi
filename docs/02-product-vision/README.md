# 02 — Product Vision

## Summary

To become the most trusted AI-powered baby naming companion for Indonesian parents — a tool that helps discover meaningful, beautiful, culturally appropriate, and unique names through intelligent conversation.

## Reasoning

Indonesia has 300+ ethnic groups, 6 major religions, and 700+ languages. No single naming tool addresses this diversity. Parents deserve a consultant that understands their cultural background, religious requirements, and personal preferences — not just a database search.

The vision positions NamAI Bayi as the entry point for Kode Mukti's AI ecosystem, establishing brand trust before expanding into broader AI parenting tools.

## Recommendation

Position NamAI Bayi as:

- **An expert consultant** — not a random generator
- **Culturally intelligent** — understands 13+ naming strategies (Islamic, Javanese, Sundanese, Balinese, Sanskrit, etc.)
- **Deeply personalized** — uses 20+ optional input fields
- **Educationally rich** — explains the meaning, philosophy, and reasoning behind every name
- **Socially viral** — built-in sharing, wishlist, and battle features

## Vision Statement

> Menjadi konsultan nama bayi AI paling tepercaya di Indonesia, membantu setiap orang tua menemukan nama yang sempurna dengan makna mendalam dan kecerdasan budaya.

(Becoming the most trusted AI baby naming consultant in Indonesia, helping every parent find the perfect name with deep meaning and cultural intelligence.)

## Implementation

| Component | Approach |
|-----------|----------|
| AI Engine | Modular provider layer (Gemini initial) |
| Platform | Android (Kotlin/Jetpack Compose) + Web (Next.js) |
| Language | Bahasa Indonesia (full interface) |
| Brand | Kode Mukti (parent brand) |
| Monetization | Freemium (Premium AI later) |

## Future Improvement

- Expand from naming consultant to full AI parenting assistant
- Build community features for name sharing and voting
- Integrate with pregnancy tracking and parenting tools
- Become the AI parenting platform under Kode Mukti
